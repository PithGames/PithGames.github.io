Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 10, 2024

At **PithGames**, we value your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and disclose information when you play our mobile game **Bubble Shooter+** (the "Game"). By using the Game, you consent to the collection and use of information as described in this policy.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information when you use the Game:

  • Usage Data: This includes information about how you interact with the Game, such as your progress, actions, and gameplay behavior.
  • Technical Data: This includes data related to the performance and technical aspects of the Game, including device type, operating system, IP address, and crash reports.

We use **Firebase** to collect and analyze this data to improve the Game's functionality, troubleshoot issues, and enhance user experience. This data is anonymized and is not used to personally identify you.

2. Advertising Data

We use **AdMob** as our ad provider to display ads (e.g., rewarded videos, interstitials, and banners). AdMob collects data related to your interactions with ads, including clicks, views, and device information. This helps to deliver personalized ads and track ad performance.

For more information on how Google AdMob handles your data, please refer to Google's AdMob Privacy Policy.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To analyze user progress and troubleshoot issues within the Game.
  • To improve the gameplay experience, fix bugs, and add new features.
  • To show advertisements that are relevant to you through AdMob.
  • To respond to support requests and provide customer service.

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not share your personal data with third parties, except in the following cases:

  • Firebase: Data collected by Firebase is used to analyze game performance and errors and may be shared with Google (Firebase’s parent company).
  • AdMob: AdMob collects data related to your interactions with ads. Please refer to AdMob's Privacy Policy for more details on how they handle your data.

5. Your Choices

We provide the following choices regarding your data:

  • Opt-out of Ads Personalization: You can control your ad personalization preferences by visiting your device’s settings or by using Google's ad settings.
  • Data Retention: We retain your data only for as long as necessary to improve the Game or to comply with legal obligations.

6. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, alteration, or destruction. However, no method of data transmission over the Internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.
